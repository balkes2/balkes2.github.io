NextLeft Indoor Navigation App

NextLeft guides visitors through even the most maze-like buildings.

The NextLeft app provides turn-by-turn directions and real time positioning for the indoors, so that visitors can navigate complex buildings with ease. The NextLeft map builder makes it easy for facility managers to edit maps in real time, so that places and routes always stay up to date.
